Chickpea Sandwich

Description


For a fast lunch recipe, look no further than this mashed chickpea sandwich. It's incredibly filling and flavourful! The sunflower seeds, pickles and bell pepper add a great crunch to this tangy mash.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (328 g) cooked chickpeas
  • 0.67 (79 g in total) bell pepper, minced
  • 0.33 (23 g in total) small red onion, minced
  • ⅓ cup + 2 Tbsp (110 mL) vegan mayonnaise, or more to taste
  • ⅓ cup (48 g) chopped pickles
  • 4 Tbsp (36 g) roasted sunflower seeds
  • 2½ Tbsp + ½ tsp (3 g) fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp (4 g) fresh chives, chopped (optional)
  • 0.67 (32 g in total) lemon, juiced (optional)
  • 1 pinch (0.4 g) salt, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch (0.1 g) ground black pepper, or more to taste
  • 8 (288 g in total) slices whole wheat bread, toasted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mash the chickpeas with a potato masher or fork until flakey.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients (except for the bread) to the bowl and mix to combine.
  3. Serve on the bread or in a wrap with your favourite fillings. Alternatively, serve as a snack with crackers, veggies, or scooped onto a salad. Enjoy!
